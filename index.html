Hello! I'm a Mathematician, currently working as an Assistant Prof. of Instruction at UT Austin. I completed my PhD in Mathematics in summer 2025 under the supervision of Sam Payne.

I have a lot of different interests, my background is in pure mathematics which a focus on combinatorics, algebraic geometry, and representation theory, though I also have a soft spot for number theory. My thesis work is concerned with applying tools from Berkovich geometry and tropical geometry to the study of algebro-geometric objects arising from algebraic groups and representation theory, such as spherical varieties. My applied math interests include data science, machine learning, cryptography, zero-knowledge proofs, and formal verification of software.
